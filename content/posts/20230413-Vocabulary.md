# ニュースで学ぶ「現代英語」

**13 Apr 2023**

### Words and Phrases

* commute --- to replace one punishment with another that is less severe
* alleged --- stated as a fact but without any proof
* involvement --- the act of taking part in something or dealing with somebody
* reaffirm --- to state something again in order to emphasize that it is still true
