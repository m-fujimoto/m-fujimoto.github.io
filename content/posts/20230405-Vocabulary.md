# ニュースで学ぶ「現代英語」

**05 Apr 2023**

### Words and Phrases

* convict --- to decide and state officially in court that somebody is guilty of a crime
* fabricate --- to invent false information in order to trick people
* serve --- to spend a period of time in prison
* pleaded --- to ask somebody for something in a very strong and serious way
* detention --- the state of being kept in a place, especially a prison, and prevented from leaving
* prosecutor --- ​a public official who charges somebody officially with a crime and prosecutes them in court
* contention --- a belief or an opinion that you express, especially in an argument
* allegedly --- expressed as though something is a fact but without giving any proof
* ferment --- to experience a chemical change because of the action of yeast or bacteria, often changing sugar to alcohol; to make something change in this way
