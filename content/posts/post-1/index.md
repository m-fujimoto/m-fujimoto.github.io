# Gaza ceasefire at risk

* Word/phrase: on the verge of\
        It means: about to happen

* Word/phrase: suspend\
        It means: delay something without being certain when or if it will continue

* Word/phrase: double down\
        It means: defend your opinion or action strongly in response to criticism
