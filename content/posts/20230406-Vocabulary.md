# News Review

****

* fraud --- the crime of cheating somebody in order to get money or goods illegally
* plead ---  to ask somebody for something in a very strong and serious way
* mugshot --- a photograph of somebody’s face kept by the police in their records to identify criminals
* humiliate --- to make somebody feel ashamed or stupid and lose the respect of other people
* unfolded --- to be gradually made known; to gradually make something known to other people
* rails against --- complains angrily

### Words and Phrases

* warrant --- a legal document that is signed by a judge and gives the police authority to do something
* deportation --- the act of forcing somebody to leave a country, usually because they have broken the law or because they have no legal right to be there
* credible --- that can be believed or trusted
* allegation --- a public statement that is made without giving proof, accusing somebody of doing something that is wrong or illegal
* jurisdiction --- an area or a country in which a particular system of laws has authority
