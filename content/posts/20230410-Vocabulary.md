# ニュースで学ぶ「現代英語」

**10 Apr 2023**

### Words and Phrases

* Deutschmark --- the former unit of money in Germany (replaced in 2002 by the euro)
* plunge --- to decrease suddenly and quickly
* spring --- to move suddenly and with one quick movement in a particular direction
* broker ---  to arrange the details of an agreement, especially between different countries
* contagion --- something bad that spreads quickly by being passed from person to person
