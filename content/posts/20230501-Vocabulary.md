# ニュースで学ぶ「現代英語」

**10 Apr 2023**

### Words and Phrases

* weigh in --- to join in a discussion, an argument, an activity, etc. by saying something important, persuading somebody, or doing something to help
* harsh --- cruel, severe and unkind
* remark --- something that you say or write which expresses an opinion, a thought, etc. about somebody/something
